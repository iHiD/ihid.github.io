Exercism

I'm the co-founder and CEO of Exercism, an education platform teaching programming languages through hands-on problem solving and mentorship. We've grown to 1.8M users by word of mouth. Our code is open source and comprised of over 350 GitHub repositories. We are a nonprofit organisation that places a strong focus on being inclusive and welcoming.

I regularly create videos that discuss our journey and provide insights in to the development of Exercism on Exercism's YouTube channel.

Kaido

Kaido is a team culture and employee wellbeing business. We help organisations take better care of their employees, and improve the culture and morale in their workplaces. We work with a diverse range of businesses from tiny startups to large multinationals such as HSBC and KPMG. Of particular personal pride is our work within the NHS helping frontline staff across the UK including internationally renowned hospitals such as Great Ormond Street Children's Hospital.

We help individuals explore how they can improve their physical health, mental health, sleep and nutrition, with a focus on sustainable and non-judgemental behaviour change.
